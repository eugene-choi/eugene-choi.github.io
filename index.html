Welcome!

I am a Member of Technical Staff at Cohere, working on large language models (LLMs). I focus on efforts on the preference modeling to provide better learning signals to Cohere's LLMs.

Previously, I worked as a natural language processing (NLP) research engineer at Reality Defender, where I developed accurate and robust systems for detecting model-generated text.

Prior to that, I was a member of the Machine Learning for Language (ML²) group, a subset of the CILVR lab, at New York University. At ML², I had the privilege of being advised by Dr. Cheolhyoung Lee and Prof. Kyunghyun Cho. Also, I am fortunate to have closely worked with Jason Phang and Prof. Sam Bowman.

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning, specifically in text generation and generative modeling.
