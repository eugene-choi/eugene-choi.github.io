Welcome! I'm a master's student studying computer science in the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences at New York University. I'm also a member of Machine Learning for Language (ML²) group, working on natural language processing research. At ML², I am fortunate to work closely with and be advised by Dr. Cheolhyoung Lee, Prof. Kyunghyun Cho, Jason Phang, and Prof. Sam Bowman.

My research interest lies in natural langauge processing and machine learning. Specifically, I am most interested in text generation and more broadly, structured prediction andgenerative modeling.

Prior to my MSc degree, I graduated from New York University (B.A. in computer science and a minor in mathematics).
