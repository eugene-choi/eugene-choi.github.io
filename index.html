Welcome!

I am a natural language processing (NLP) research engineer at Reality Defender, where I focus on developing accurate and robust systems for detecting model-generated text.

I am also a member of the Machine Learning for Language (ML²) group, a subset of the CILVR lab, at New York University. At ML², I have the privilege of being advised by Dr. Cheolhyoung Lee and Prof. Kyunghyun Cho. Also, I am fortunate to have closely worked with Jason Phang and Prof. Sam Bowman.

My research interests lie at the intersection of natural language processing and machine learning. Currently, I am most interested in text generation problems and, more broadly, generative modeling.
